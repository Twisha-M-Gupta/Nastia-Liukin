Nastia Liukin

She is a Russian American gymnast. She is 31 years old, born in October 30, 1889 in russia.

Career

She started gymnastics at the age of three. She has 9 olimpic medals, 4 ofthem silver and 5 of them olimpic gold. In 2018 she joins the International gymnastics hall of fame. She represented the United States at three World Championships and one Olympic Games. In October 2011, Liukin announced that she was returning to gymnastics with the hopes of making a second Olympic team.

Childhood

Nasita, at the age of three because she was "always hanging around in the gym" with her parents, who could not afford a babysitter to look after her while they were working as coaches. Liukin's parents initially did not aspire for their daughter to become a gymnast, knowing the pressure of high-level competition firsthand, but relented when they noticed her aptitude for the sport.

Retiring

Liukin did not make the 2012 Olympic team, after several falls at the Olympic Trials and other pre-Olympic events, and retired from the sport in 2012. She was 22 when she retired.

After retiring / Now

She was pretty young when she retired and had a long life after that. She became an actor and is know for Stick it, Hellcats and Make it or brake it.
